Club Med GPT is an AI assistant dedicated to all Club Med employees. It answers your questions in natural language, helps you generate or summarize text, and can even translate content into multiple languages. All in a secure Club Med environment to protect you and our organization’s data.

How to Get Started

  1. Connect: From any browser, go to https://clubmedgpt.ai.clubmed.com/ and select “Connexion with Microsoft.”
  2. Authenticate: Enter your usual Club Med login and password.
  3. Begin Chatting: Create a new conversation, type your prompt in natural language, or use voice input if you prefer.
  4. Enhance Your Prompts: Upload files, images, or enable web search for real-time information.
  5. Manage History: Rename conversation titles, add custom labels, and share with colleagues securely.

Prompting Best Practices

A detailed prompt avoids incorrect or out-of-context responses. Provide context, use examples, and specify the desired format to get the best results. Below are the 6 commandments for effective prompting:

  • 1. Specify the format: “Please provide a bulleted list.”
  • 2. Give examples: “Summarize the text using the structure of Example 1.”
  • 3. Give context: “You're an insurance lawyer explaining this to a layperson.”
  • 4. Include steps: “First list all resorts, then filter only those suitable for 3-year-olds.”
  • 5. Put limits: “List only 5 examples, excluding mountain resorts.”
  • 6. Reformulate: If results aren’t correct, rephrase your query for clarity.
